Our Medical Services

At St. Josephine Mara Hospital we offer affordable health services to humanity — a 24-hour Level 4 hospital serving Naivasha and beyond.

🏥

In & Outpatient

Fully equipped outpatient with a competent team of doctors and nurses responding to emergencies, consultations and general care.

🤰

Maternity

Fully equipped maternity unit with modern equipment, specialist gynaecologist, paediatrician and 24-hour skilled delivery care.

🔪

Major & Minor Surgeries

Fully equipped operating theatre with modern machinery for emergency and general surgeries, including specialised procedures.

📡

Ultrasound & X-ray

Modern imaging machines for ultrasound, ECG, ECHO, X-ray and Doppler ultrasound operated by experienced radiologists.

🔬

Laboratory Services

24-hour fully equipped laboratory with state-of-the-art equipment and competent personnel to give accurate results at affordable prices.

🩺

Endoscopy & ECG

Advanced endoscopy procedures and ECG diagnostics carried out by our experienced team of specialist consultants.

👶

Antenatal & Postnatal

Comprehensive antenatal care, postnatal follow-up, family planning and immunisation services for mothers and children.

💊

Pharmacy

24-hour pharmacy fully stocked with a wide variety of drugs to respond to all client and patient needs at affordable prices.

🦷

Dental & Optical

Dental consultation, treatment, and optical services delivered by qualified professionals in a comfortable clinical setting.

🚑

Ambulance Services

Round-the-clock emergency ambulance services ensuring fast, safe patient transport with trained paramedic support.

Immunization

Full immunization schedules for children and adults, including travel vaccines and national immunization programme services.

Our Story

A Legacy of Healing in the Heart of Kenya

Founded in 2023 by the Josephine Mara Foundation, our hospital began as a small clinic serving Naivasha's underserved communities. Today we stand as a 10+ bed regional centre, equipped with technologies that rival the finest hospitals in East Africa — yet grounded in the same spirit of compassionate service.
